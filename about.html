About Us

SarLata IT Solutions and Training leads through innovation and pursuing excellence. The name SarLata has been inspired from two great ladies Sarala Kanchan and Premalatha Shetty, who have been influential in the early career growth of the founder members.
  • Our Vision is to empower, inspire and transform lives through quality education
  • Our Mission is to provide the best flexible learning experience to our students.
  • Our core value is to encourage an independent learning environment that empowers students to embody responsibility and accountability.
  • Our Aim is to build bridges of knowledge and push boundaries by producing great talent.

Having various field related experiences, we believe a learner’s aim should have unlimited possibilities. Everyone has an opportunity to pursue their aspiration through effective teaching methods leveraging the latest technologies.
We dare to dream while setting international educational standards for our students and colleagues.
