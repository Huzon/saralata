About Us

SarLata Academy leads through innovation and pursuing excellence. Vision is to empower, inspire and transform lives through quality education Mission is to provide the best flexible learning experience to our students.
Having various field related experiences, we believe a learner’s aim should have possibilities unlimited. Everyone has an opportunity to pursue their aspiration through effective teaching methods leveraging the latest technologies. Our core value is to encourage an independent learning environment that empowers students to embody responsibility and accountability.
SarLata dares to dream while setting international educational standards for our students and colleagues. Aim to build bridges of knowledge and push boundaries by producing great talent. The name SarLata has been inspired from two great ladies Sarala Kanchan and Premalatha Shetty, who have been influential in the early career growth of the founder members.
